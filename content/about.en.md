As a research fellow at the CNRS, I work at the [Institute of Functional Genomics](https://www.igf.cnrs.fr/) in Montpellier. My research focuses on neuromodulators such as dopamine (as well as norepinephrine and acetylcholine), neuronal plasticity (synaptic and intrinsic), and decision-making. I build theoretical models (neural networks, reinforcement learning) constrained by experimental data and test the models’ predictions, particularly in rodents (electrophysiology, pharmacogenetics, optogenetics, imaging). I collaborate with theoretical colleagues (mathematics, physics, computer science, robotics) and experimentalists (in vitro and in vivo approaches).
